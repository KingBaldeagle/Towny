## Notifications

Various machines can give you messages that are sent to your personal notifications. Some of these notifications will be sent to your chat box, while all will be accessible in the Notifications Screen. The Notification Screen can be accessed by clicking on the !!! button shown on the top-right corner of your inventory. If you have an unread notification, this button will have orange !!! to alert you about the new notification(s).

![](https://i.imgur.com/DRaJ2wp.png)

### Notification Screen

The Notification Screen is a simple screen that shows a log of all notifications you've received, with a tab for each notification category/source for easy filtering. The default "General" tab shows all notifications regardless of their source as shown below:

![](https://i.imgur.com/70yChJx.png)

Whereas each categories tab will only display notifications from that specific source, and a "Delete" button will appear on the right of the notification allowing you to clear old and unwanted notifications:

![](https://i.imgur.com/jxiHLG7.png)

All "unread" notifications are displayed with a yellow background, while read notifications are a standard gray. Clicking the "Mark as Read" button in the top-right corner will mark all notifications from the selected category.
